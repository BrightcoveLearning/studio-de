Richtlinien zur Inhaltssicherheit

Die aktuellen CSPs von Brightcove:

default-src 'self'; script-src 'self' players.brightcove.net vjs.zencdn.net; connect-src 'self' players.brightcove.net edge.api.brightcove.com; img-src 'self' players.brightcove.net *.boltdns.net *.akamaihd.net; style-src 'self' players.brightcove.net; frame-src 'self' players.brightcove.net; media-src 'self' *.brightcovecdn.com *.boltdns.net *.media.brightcove.com *.llnw.net *.llnwd.net *.akafms.net *.akamaihd.net *.cf.brightcove.com; prefetch-src 'self' *.boltdns.net;
